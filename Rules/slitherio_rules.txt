Rules of Slither.io:

You are a small snake that needs to survive in a world by eating the food that spawns

Move your mouse to move and the snake will follow the cursor

If your head collides with the body of another snake you die so make sure to look out for other snakes while scourging for food
when someone else collides with your body, they die and spawn more food that you can eat

You must evade other snakes and try to eat as much food as you can

Right click your mouse to get a boost of speed but be careful as you may collide with another snake

Playing this game helps you improve yopur reflexes and concentration

Have Fun!
